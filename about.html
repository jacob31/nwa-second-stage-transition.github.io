About

NWA Second Stage Transition help encourage transformation to individuals who are willing to break the chain of bondage to find hope and take control of theirs lives. We seek to strengthen linkages between residents and community-based resources to prevent the cycle of abuse, addiction and homelessness.

Mission

NWA Second Stage Transition will provide a safe and supportive home to individuals who want to continue to succeed in an after-care substance abuse recovery, domestic violence recovery, sexual abuse recovery or a workforce program.

Vision

We envision acceptance, empowerment, hope, love, accountability and informed decision making.

Values

Professionalism, Confidentiality, Authenticity, Integrity, Inclusivitivity, Respect.

Goals

  • Will be accepted base on eligibility- a complete full phase of substance abuse treatment from a treatment center.
  • Residents will continue to participate in an after-care recovery services program, counseling/art therapy, demotic and sexual violence prevention and parenting classes.
  • Residents will work on financial budgeting/life skills for a long term and employment.
  • They will attend, support group, health and wellness classes, an ongoing case management and support will help transition residents into permanent housing/community upon graduation,
  • After graduation, a graduate will be welcome to continue to be part of NWA Second Stage transition outreach services.
