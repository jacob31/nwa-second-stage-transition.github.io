Programs

Phase 1 is a short-term emergency housing (1-2 weeks) service for individuals who have completed a recent half-way residential housing, shelter, or treatment program, but are awaiting approval to get into an apartment.

Phase 2 Second Stage Transition seeks to provide a phase 2 safe and supportive short-term residential housing (Six months) service to women who want to live self-sufficiently safer lives; make informed decisions and undergo personal growth.

Residential Manifesto

  • Reduce the cycle of homelessness and unhealthy lifestyles through life skills and other programs.
  • Acceptance into the NWA Second Stage Transition shall base on eligibility.
  • Shall assist individuals or families in continuing the process through collaboration with halfway houses and other community resources.
